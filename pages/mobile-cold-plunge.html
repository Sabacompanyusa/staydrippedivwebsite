Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about mobile cold plunge therapy

Is cold plunging safe for beginners?

Yes, when done with proper guidance. Our trained professionals ensure safe introduction to cold therapy with gradual temperature progression and continuous monitoring.

How cold is the water?

We typically start beginners at 50-55¬∞F and progress to 38-45¬∞F for experienced users. Temperature is precisely controlled and adjusted based on your comfort and goals.

How long should I stay in the cold plunge?

Beginners start with 1-3 minutes, progressing to 5-10 minutes for experienced users. Duration depends on temperature, individual tolerance, and therapeutic goals.

What should I bring or wear?

Bring a swimsuit and we provide everything else including towels. We recommend avoiding alcohol beforehand and eating a light meal 1-2 hours prior.

Do you service all areas?

We serve the greater Phoenix metro area and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location.

Can I do cold plunging if I have health conditions?

Cold therapy has contraindications for certain conditions. We conduct health screenings and recommend consulting your physician if you have cardiovascular, respiratory, or other medical concerns.
