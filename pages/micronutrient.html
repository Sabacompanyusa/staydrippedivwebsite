Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about micronutrient testing

How is this different from regular blood tests?

Our cellular micronutrient testing measures how vitamins and minerals function inside your cells, not just their blood levels. This provides a more accurate picture of your nutritional status.

Do I need to stop supplements before testing?

No, continue taking your supplements as normal. The test measures your cells' actual functional nutrient levels over the past 4-6 months, not recent intake.

How often should I get tested?

We typically recommend retesting every 6-12 months to assess progress and adjust supplementation. Initial follow-up testing may be done after 3-4 months of supplementation.

Can this test detect food sensitivities?

This specific test focuses on nutrient function. We offer separate food sensitivity testing that can be combined with micronutrient analysis for comprehensive evaluation.

Will this test show if my supplements are working?

Yes, the cellular testing will show whether your supplements are actually improving your nutritional status at the cellular level, not just raising blood levels.

Is the test covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by insurance plan. We provide detailed receipts for insurance submission and offer competitive self-pay pricing for this specialized testing.
