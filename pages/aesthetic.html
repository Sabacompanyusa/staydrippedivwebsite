Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about aesthetic treatments

How long do Botox results last?

Botox results typically last 3-4 months. With regular treatments, some patients experience longer-lasting results as the muscles become trained to relax.

Are dermal fillers safe?

Yes, when administered by qualified professionals, FDA-approved dermal fillers are very safe. We use only premium brands with established safety profiles.

How much downtime is required?

Most treatments require minimal to no downtime. Injectables may cause mild swelling or bruising for 1-3 days, while laser treatments may require 3-7 days of healing.

When will I see results?

Botox results appear in 3-7 days, fillers are immediate with final results in 2 weeks, and skin treatments show gradual improvement over 4-6 weeks.

Can treatments be combined?

Yes, many treatments complement each other beautifully. We often recommend combination approaches for optimal results and can space treatments appropriately.

What should I expect during my consultation?

Your consultation includes a thorough skin analysis, discussion of your goals, treatment recommendations, and detailed explanation of procedures and expected outcomes.
