Membership FAQs

Common questions about our membership program

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can change your membership level at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we'll help you transition smoothly.

Do unused services roll over?

Complimentary services included in your membership do not roll over month to month, but your discounts apply to all services you book throughout your membership.

Is there a contract commitment?

No long-term contracts required. You can cancel your membership with 30 days notice at any time. We believe in earning your loyalty through excellent service.

Can I share my membership benefits?

Membership benefits are non-transferable and can only be used by the member. However, you can refer friends and family for special referral benefits.

What if I travel frequently?

Our mobile service area and telehealth consultations accommodate busy lifestyles. Platinum members have access to 24/7 virtual support wherever you are.

Are there family membership options?

Yes! Contact us about family plans that provide benefits for multiple household members with special pricing and shared appointment coordination.
