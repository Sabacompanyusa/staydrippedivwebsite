Membership FAQs

Common questions about our membership program

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can change your membership level at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do unused services roll over?

Complimentary services included in your membership do not roll over month to month, but discounts apply to all services you book.

Is there a contract commitment?

No long-term contracts required. You can cancel your membership with 30 days notice at any time.

Can I share my membership benefits?

Membership benefits are non-transferable and can only be used by the member. However, you can refer friends for special benefits.

What happens if I travel frequently?

Our telehealth consultations and flexible scheduling accommodate busy lifestyles. Platinum members have access to 24/7 virtual support.

Are there family membership options?

Yes! Contact us about family plans that provide benefits for multiple household members with special pricing.
