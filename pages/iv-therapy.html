Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IV therapy

How long does an IV therapy session take?

Most IV sessions take 30-60 minutes, depending on the specific formulation and your body's needs. NAD+ treatments may take longer.

How often should I get IV therapy?

Frequency depends on your health goals. Some clients benefit from weekly sessions, while others prefer monthly maintenance. We'll create a personalized schedule during your consultation.

Are there any side effects?

IV therapy is generally very safe with minimal side effects. Some people may experience slight bruising at the injection site or a cool sensation during infusion.

Can I eat before my IV session?

Yes, we recommend eating a light meal before your session to prevent any lightheadedness during treatment.

Will I feel results immediately?

Many clients feel increased energy and improved well-being within hours of treatment. Results may vary based on your individual health status and the specific formulation.

Do you accept insurance?

IV therapy is typically not covered by insurance as it's considered elective wellness treatment. We offer flexible payment options and membership discounts.
