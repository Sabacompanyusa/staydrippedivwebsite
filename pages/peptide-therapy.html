What Are Peptides?

Understanding the science behind these powerful therapeutic compounds

The Building Blocks of Life

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in your body. They instruct cells to perform specific functions like healing, growth, fat burning, and anti-aging processes. As we age, our natural peptide production declines, making therapeutic peptides an effective way to restore optimal function.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about peptide therapy

Are peptides safe?

When prescribed and monitored by medical professionals, peptides are generally very safe with minimal side effects. We use only pharmaceutical-grade peptides from certified facilities.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary by peptide and individual. Some benefits like improved sleep or mood may be noticed within days, while tissue repair and body composition changes typically take 4-8 weeks.

Do I need to inject peptides myself?

Most peptides require subcutaneous injection, which we thoroughly train you to do safely at home. The needles are very small and most patients find it easier than expected.

Can peptides be combined with other treatments?

Yes, peptides often complement other therapies like hormone replacement, IV therapy, and weight management programs. We coordinate all treatments for optimal results.

How long do I need to use peptides?

Treatment duration varies based on goals. Some protocols are 6-12 weeks for specific outcomes, while others may be used longer-term for ongoing benefits.

Are peptides legal?

Yes, peptides prescribed by licensed physicians for therapeutic purposes are legal. We only use peptides approved for research and therapeutic use.
