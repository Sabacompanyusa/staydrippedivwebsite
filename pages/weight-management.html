Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our weight management program

Who is a good candidate for GLP-1 medications?

Good candidates typically have a BMI of 30+ or BMI 27+ with weight-related health conditions. Our medical team will evaluate your individual situation during consultation.

What are the common side effects?

Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and constipation, which typically improve as your body adjusts. Our team provides strategies to minimize these effects.

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Most patients lose 15-25% of their body weight over 12-18 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, medication response, and adherence to the program.

Is the weight loss permanent?

Weight loss can be maintained with continued lifestyle changes. Some patients continue medication long-term while others transition to maintenance strategies we develop together.

Does insurance cover the program?

Coverage varies by insurance plan. We provide detailed receipts for insurance submission and offer flexible payment options for out-of-pocket expenses.

How often will I need appointments?

Frequency depends on your chosen program, ranging from monthly (Essential) to weekly (Premium) visits, with additional support available as needed.
