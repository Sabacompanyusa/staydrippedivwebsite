What is NAD+?

Understanding the science behind this revolutionary anti-aging therapy

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide)

NAD+ is a critical coenzyme found in every cell of your body that plays a fundamental role in energy production, DNA repair, and cellular regeneration. As we age, NAD+ levels naturally decline by up to 50% by age 50, contributing to the aging process and age-related health issues.

How often should I receive NAD+ treatments?

Can NAD+ be combined with other treatments?
