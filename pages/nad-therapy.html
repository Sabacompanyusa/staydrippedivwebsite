What is NAD+?

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a critical coenzyme found in every cell of your body that plays a fundamental role in energy production, DNA repair, and cellular regeneration. As we age, NAD+ levels naturally decline by up to 50% by age 50, contributing to the aging process and age-related health issues.
