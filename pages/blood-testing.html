Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about blood testing

Do I need to fast before my blood test?

Fasting requirements depend on the specific tests ordered. Most panels require 8-12 hours of fasting. We'll provide specific instructions when you schedule.

How long do results take?

Most standard panels are available within 24-48 hours. Specialized tests may take 3-5 business days. You'll receive secure online access to your results.

Are the tests covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by insurance plan and medical necessity. We provide detailed receipts for insurance submission and offer competitive self-pay pricing.

Can I add tests to my panel?

Yes, additional tests can often be added to your order before sample collection. Our team will help customize your panel based on your needs.

What happens after I get my results?

We schedule a consultation to review your results, explain findings, and create a personalized action plan for your health optimization goals.

How often should I get tested?

Testing frequency depends on your health status and goals. Generally, annual comprehensive testing is recommended, with more frequent monitoring for specific conditions.
