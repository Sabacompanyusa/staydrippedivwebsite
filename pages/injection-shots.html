Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about vitamin injection shots

Do vitamin shots hurt?

Most clients experience minimal discomfort. We use small needles and the injection takes only seconds. Any soreness typically subsides within a few hours.

How often should I get vitamin shots?

Frequency depends on the specific vitamin and your individual needs. B12 shots can be given weekly to monthly, while others may be less frequent. We'll recommend the best schedule for you.

Can I get multiple shots in one visit?

Yes, many clients combine shots for enhanced benefits. Our medical team will ensure safe combinations and proper spacing if needed.

Are there any side effects?

Side effects are rare and typically mild, such as temporary soreness at the injection site. Our medical team reviews your health history to ensure safety.

How quickly will I feel results?

Many people feel increased energy within 30 minutes to a few hours. B12 shots often provide immediate energy, while other benefits may develop over days.

Can I exercise after getting a shot?

Yes, you can resume normal activities immediately. Some people prefer getting shots before workouts for enhanced energy and performance.
