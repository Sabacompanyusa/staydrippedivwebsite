Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile IV therapy services

Mobile IV therapy brings professional intravenous treatments directly to your location. Our licensed medical professionals administer IV fluids, vitamins, minerals, and medications in the comfort of your home, office, or chosen venue.
Most IV treatments take 30-60 minutes, depending on the specific therapy and your individual needs. NAD+ treatments may take longer (2-4 hours) due to the nature of the therapy.
Yes, all treatments are administered by licensed medical professionals (RNs, NPs, or MDs) using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients and sterile equipment. We follow strict safety protocols and conduct health assessments before each treatment.
We serve the entire Phoenix Valley including Scottsdale, Phoenix, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Glendale, Peoria, and surrounding areas. Same-day service is available in most locations.
Treatment costs vary by service: IV hydration starts at $150, NAD+ therapy starts at $300, peptide therapy starts at $250, and vitamin injections start at $50. Membership plans offer significant savings for regular users.
Most IV wellness treatments are not covered by insurance as they are considered elective/cosmetic. However, we provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your HSA/FSA or insurance for potential reimbursement.
