Frequently Asked Questions

What is mobile IV therapy?

Mobile IV therapy brings professional intravenous treatments directly to your location. Our certified nurses administer IV drips containing vitamins, minerals, and hydration in the comfort of your home, office, or hotel.

How long does a treatment take?

Most IV therapy sessions take 30-60 minutes, depending on the type of treatment and your individual needs.

Is IV therapy safe?

Yes, when administered by qualified healthcare professionals. All our nurses are licensed and experienced in IV therapy administration.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept HSA and FSA payments. While most insurance doesn't cover elective IV therapy, we can provide documentation for reimbursement requests.
