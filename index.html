Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile IV therapy services, safety protocols, and booking process.

What is mobile IV therapy?

Mobile IV therapy brings professional intravenous treatments directly to your location. Our certified nurses administer IV drips containing vitamins, minerals, and hydration in the comfort of your home, office, or hotel. This convenient service eliminates travel time and provides personalized care in your preferred environment.

How long does a treatment take?

Most IV therapy sessions take 30-60 minutes, depending on the type of treatment and your individual needs. Our nurses will discuss the expected duration during your consultation and ensure you're comfortable throughout the entire process.

Is IV therapy safe?

Yes, when administered by qualified healthcare professionals. All our nurses are licensed, experienced, and follow strict safety protocols. We use sterile equipment, pharmaceutical-grade ingredients, and conduct thorough health screenings before each treatment.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept HSA and FSA payments for eligible treatments. While most insurance doesn't cover elective IV therapy, we can provide detailed receipts and documentation for potential reimbursement requests. Contact your insurance provider to verify coverage.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Scottsdale, Phoenix, and surrounding areas in Arizona. Our mobile service brings premium IV therapy directly to your home, office, hotel, or event location within our service area.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Stay hydrated before your appointment and eat a light meal. Wear comfortable clothing with easy access to your arm. Our nurses will bring all necessary equipment and will guide you through any specific preparation based on your chosen treatment.
