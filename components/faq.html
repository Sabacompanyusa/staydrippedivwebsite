Health & Safety

Most healthy adults can safely receive IV therapy. We conduct a health screening before treatment and may require medical clearance for certain conditions. Pregnant women, individuals with heart conditions, kidney disease, or other medical conditions should consult their physician first.

We follow strict medical protocols including sterile technique, single-use equipment, proper medical waste disposal, and comprehensive health assessments. All staff are licensed medical professionals with current certifications in IV therapy and emergency response.

Frequency depends on your individual needs and health goals. Many clients benefit from monthly treatments, while others prefer weekly sessions during stress or illness recovery. Our medical team will recommend an appropriate schedule based on your wellness objectives.
